Motivated graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and visiting students
interested in gravitational-wave physics, relativistic astrophysics, data
analysis, statistics, computation, or machine learning are welcome to contact
Prof. Yan Wang.

Applicants are encouraged to include a short research statement, CV,
transcript, and any relevant coding or research experience.

## Backgrounds of Interest

- Physics, astronomy, or applied mathematics
- Programming and scientific computing
- Bayesian inference, statistics, or signal processing
- Numerical relativity or compact-object astrophysics
