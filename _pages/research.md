The group works on relativistic astrophysics, gravitational-wave astrophysics,
and gravitational-wave data analysis. The page below is a first structure for
the public website and can be expanded with projects, selected papers, student
topics, and software links.

## Relativistic Astrophysics

We study astrophysical systems in strong gravity, especially compact objects and
binary systems relevant to gravitational-wave observations.

## Gravitational-Wave Astrophysics

We investigate the astrophysical interpretation of gravitational-wave sources,
including compact-object binaries and their connection to broader
multi-messenger astronomy.

## Gravitational-Wave Data Analysis

We develop and apply methods for signal modeling, detection, parameter
estimation, and statistical inference in gravitational-wave data.

## Future Observatories

We are interested in science cases and data-analysis methods for current and
next-generation ground-based and space-based gravitational-wave detectors.
